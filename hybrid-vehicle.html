Introduction
Getting bored of learning century old concepts? Want yourself to adapt with evolving technologies in electrical field? We are here to offer you a platform to answer the question "Can you run the whole world without oil?" as an electrical engineer. Only 15% of the fuel energy is used to move your vehicle, the rest is lost to engine and driveline inefficiencies. As many interdisciplinary inventions are giving solutions to existing problems, there is one such kind which is becoming a boon to future generations. The solution is "HYBRID ELECTRIC VEHICLE". It combines the conventional internal combustion engine propulsion system with an electric propulsion system to achieve better fuel economy and better performance with advance electric system. Grab a chance to learn this emerging technology and become a master of this world.
