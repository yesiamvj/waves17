Introduction
Have you ever been fascinated by the way an ant hill works? Every single one of the ants work in a systematic and united way to create progress. Alone an ant is considered insignificant but an army of ants! Now that is a formidable force. Imagine if we could design robots with the behaviour of ants where they could communicate with each other and do things in a systematic way, we can automate entire industries by such robots and these robots with a single hive mind are swarm robots.
Learn to construct a swarm bot and interact with bots built by other teams to perform a series of tasks. We are here with an expert team with hands-on experience of this technology of the future. Hop in for a fun and beginner friendly workshop which will give you a wonderful experience on the world of robotics.
Agenda
Day 1

  • Gyro sensors and rotary sensor explanation

  • Movement pattern algorithm
